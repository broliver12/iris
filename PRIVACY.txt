Iris: Privacy Policy (c) 2022

This is an open source Android app developed for personal interest by Oliver Straszynski.

The source code is available on GitHub under the MIT license.
The app is available on Google Play.

This app does not, and will never, collect any personal data.
This app does not, and will never, sell any data to a 3rd party.
This app does not track your location.
There is not currently any form of analytics implemented in this app.

All colors data is stored on your phone, and will be permanently lost if the app is deleted, or the data is cleared.
